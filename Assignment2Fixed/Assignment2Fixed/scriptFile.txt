You arrive at Lillebrook, a small town at the south end of the Great Basin.  To your northwest is an ominous forest; the trees tremble menacingly in the wind.  To the northeast are the rolling hills of Varben, known for their relative peace and goodwill, as far as rolling hills go.  You stand directly north of the town gates.  Your mercenary ways have led you here, at the request of a letter, saying nothing more than a man named John urgently requested the help of a traveling sword.  What would you like to do?/
